FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How much do your services cost?

Our pricing starts at ₹25,000/month for our Starter package and goes up to ₹1,00,000+/month for Enterprise solutions. We also offer custom packages tailored to your specific needs and budget. All pricing is transparent with no hidden fees.

Do you work with businesses outside India?

Yes! While we specialize in the Indian market, we work with clients globally. Our team is experienced in creating strategies that work across different regions and cultures. We handle time zones efficiently and communicate in English, Hindi, and other regional languages.

What industries do you work with?

We've successfully worked with businesses across various industries including E-commerce, Healthcare, Real Estate, Education, Food & Beverage, Fashion, Technology, Finance, and more. Our strategies are customized for each industry's unique needs.

How long does it take to see results?

While some metrics like engagement can improve within the first month, substantial business results typically appear within 3-6 months. Social media marketing is a long-term strategy. We provide detailed monthly reports so you can track progress every step of the way.

Do you offer contracts or can I cancel anytime?

We typically recommend a 3-month minimum commitment to see meaningful results. After that, we work on a monthly rolling basis. You can cancel with 30 days notice. We believe in earning your business every month through exceptional results.

Will I have a dedicated account manager?

Yes! Every client gets a dedicated account manager who serves as your primary point of contact. For Enterprise clients, you'll also have direct access to our senior strategists and creative team. We believe in building strong partnerships.

What makes you different from other agencies?

Three things: (1) Deep understanding of the Indian market combined with global best practices (2) Data-driven approach with transparent reporting (3) We focus on business outcomes, not just vanity metrics. Our success is measured by your growth.

Do you provide content in regional languages?

Absolutely! We create content in Hindi, English, and can accommodate other regional languages like Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi, and more. We understand that regional language content often performs exceptionally well in India.
